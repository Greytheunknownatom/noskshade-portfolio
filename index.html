About

Design that earns attention, then earns trust.

Senior Product Designer with 5+ years turning startup ambition into interfaces that actually ship. I work at the intersection of strategy, craft, and code — from MVP sketches to full design systems.

My sweet spot is early-stage products where complexity lives beneath the surface and the job is making it feel effortless for the people who use it. I work with early-stage founders, brand builders, and product teams who need a designer that moves fast, thinks in systems, and ships work that actually changes the numbers.
